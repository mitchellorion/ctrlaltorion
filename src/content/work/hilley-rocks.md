Hilley Rocks is a complete brand and storefront for a small crystal business — "objects of quiet magic." Beyond the editorial shop design, it includes the behind-the-scenes tooling: a photo and listing pipeline, an own-checkout store, and a synced sales channel so inventory stays consistent across platforms.
